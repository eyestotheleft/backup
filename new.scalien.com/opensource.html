Overview

ScalienDB follows a dual open source - commercial licensing model. The core ScalienDB database server is distributed under the GNU Affero GPL (AGPL) open source license. The ScalienDB client libraries are distributed under the open source BSD license to allow its use in any commercial product. Additional enterprise modules such as backup and restore tools, support contracts are available under a commercial license.

This allows casual and small business users to use ScalienDB at no charge and sign up for additional licenses once the business demands commercial support or enterprise level features. Enterprise users are free to evaluate and use ScalienDB under the AGPL license for proof of concept systems.

View the open source licenses:

  • GNU Affero GPL license - ScalienDB server
  • BSD license - ScalienDB client libraries
