FAQ

Is Keyspace a scalable system?

Keyspace is a replicated system, meaning all nodes store the same data. In the sense of storage-space, it is not scalable.

Keyspace is licensed under the AGPL license, what does that mean?

The Keyspace C/C++ source is licensed to you under the Affero GPL licence. For more, see the Wikipedia entry on AGPL. If you are a commercial entity in need of custom licensing, please contact us.

Note that the Python and PHP client libraries are under the permissive BSD license, meaning you can link them freely against your programs.
