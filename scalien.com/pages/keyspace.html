Keyspace features at a glance


Consistent replication
Keyspace servers are always in-sync and appear as a single key-value database
High availability
Keyspace masks server and network failures and appears as a single, highly available service
Fault tolerant
Servers can fail without data loss or service outages thanks to replication and automatic fail-over
High performance
Keyspace can handle up to 100.000 replicated operations per second
Upgrade path
Upgrade from single-server Keyspace configurations to replicated configurations seamlessly
